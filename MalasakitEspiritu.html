About Malasakit by - Iverson

Our Mission

Malasakit by - Iverson is a government-backed healthcare platform designed to revolutionize how Filipinos access medical services. Born from the spirit of "Malasakit" - compassionate care for all - our platform bridges the gap between patients and healthcare providers across the Philippines.

What We Do

We eliminate the barriers that prevent Filipinos from accessing quality healthcare. Through our digital platform, we connect patients with doctors, streamline laboratory appointments, provide financial assistance applications, and offer hospitalization support - all completely free of charge.

Our Vision

A Philippines where every Filipino has equal access to quality healthcare, regardless of their economic status or geographic location. We envision a future where technology serves as the bridge to better health outcomes for all.

Government Partnership

Malasakit by - Iverson is proudly supported by the Philippine government in partnership with the Department of Health, Philippine Health Insurance Corporation (PhilHealth), and local government units nationwide. Together, we're building a healthier Philippines.
